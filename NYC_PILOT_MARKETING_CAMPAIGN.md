# NYC Pilot Marketing Campaign - OnPurpose Launch Strategy

## 🎯 Campaign Overview

**Objective:** Recruit 50 verified NYC hosts across 5 categories within 30 days  
**Target Revenue:** $3,000/month platform fees (100 bookings at $30 avg commission)  
**Launch Timeline:** 4-week intensive recruitment and marketing blitz  

---

## 📊 Host Recruitment Targets by Category

### **Local Experts** (15 hosts) - $35-50/hour
- **Target Profile:** Food bloggers, neighborhood natives, local business owners
- **Recruitment Channels:** Food Instagram accounts, local Facebook groups, Yelp Elite
- **Value Proposition:** Monetize local knowledge, flexible scheduling

### **Cultural Guides** (10 hosts) - $40-60/hour  
- **Target Profile:** Licensed tour guides, museum docents, history enthusiasts
- **Recruitment Channels:** NYC tour guide associations, cultural institutions
- **Value Proposition:** Supplement tour income, share passion for NYC history

### **Wellness Coaches** (10 hosts) - $45-65/hour
- **Target Profile:** Yoga instructors, fitness trainers, meditation teachers
- **Recruitment Channels:** Yoga studios, fitness centers, wellness events
- **Value Proposition:** Expand client base, outdoor/unique venue opportunities

### **Creative Mentors** (10 hosts) - $50-75/hour
- **Target Profile:** Professional artists, photographers, designers, writers
- **Recruitment Channels:** Art galleries, creative co-working spaces, design meetups
- **Value Proposition:** Teaching income, portfolio exposure, networking

### **Professional Networkers** (5 hosts) - $55-75/hour
- **Target Profile:** Startup founders, executives, industry leaders
- **Recruitment Channels:** LinkedIn, networking events, accelerators
- **Value Proposition:** Give back to community, expand professional network
