# ⚖️ Phase 4: Legal Documentation & Compliance

## 📋 **Step 10: Legal Framework**

### Terms of Service
**Platform Terms**:
- **Service description**: OnPurpose hospitality platform
- **User responsibilities**: Guest and host obligations
- **Payment terms**: Fees, refunds, dispute resolution
- **Liability limitations**: Platform liability caps
- **Intellectual property**: Content ownership rights
- **Termination clauses**: Account suspension/deletion

### Privacy Policy
**Data Protection**:
- **Data collection**: What information we collect
- **Data usage**: How we use personal information
- **Data sharing**: Third-party integrations (Stripe, SendGrid)
- **User rights**: Access, deletion, portability
- **Cookies**: Tracking and analytics
- **GDPR/CCPA compliance**: International privacy laws

### Host Agreement
**Host-Specific Terms**:
- **Service standards**: Quality expectations
- **Background checks**: Verification requirements
- **Insurance requirements**: Liability coverage
- **Tax responsibilities**: 1099 reporting, tax obligations
- **Cancellation policies**: Host cancellation penalties
- **Dispute resolution**: Mediation and arbitration

### Guest Agreement
**Guest Protection**:
- **Booking policies**: Confirmation, changes, cancellations
- **Safety guidelines**: Personal safety recommendations
- **Refund policies**: Full/partial refund conditions
- **Behavior standards**: Respectful interaction requirements
- **Dispute process**: Issue reporting and resolution

## 🛡️ **Safety & Insurance**

### Safety Protocols
**Risk Management**:
- **Host verification**: Background checks, references
- **Meeting guidelines**: Public spaces, safety tips
- **Emergency procedures**: Contact information, protocols
- **Incident reporting**: Safety issue documentation
- **Insurance coverage**: General liability protection

### Insurance Requirements
**Coverage Types**:
- **General liability**: $1M coverage minimum for hosts
- **Professional liability**: Service-related claims
- **Platform insurance**: OnPurpose umbrella policy
- **Property damage**: Protection for host/guest property
- **Medical coverage**: Accident/injury protection

### Legal Documents Required
```
1. Terms of Service (Platform)
2. Privacy Policy (GDPR/CCPA compliant)
3. Host Service Agreement
4. Guest Booking Agreement
5. Payment Processing Terms
6. Cookie Policy
7. Community Guidelines
8. Safety & Security Policy
9. Dispute Resolution Policy
10. Intellectual Property Policy
```

### Compliance Checklist
- **Business registration**: LLC/Corp formation
- **Tax ID**: Federal and state tax registration
- **Business licenses**: NYC business permits
- **Sales tax**: Collection and remittance setup
- **Employment law**: If hiring employees
- **ADA compliance**: Accessibility requirements
- **Consumer protection**: State and federal regulations
