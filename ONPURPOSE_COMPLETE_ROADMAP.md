# 🚀 OnPurpose Complete Development Roadmap

## ✅ **Current Status: LIVE & OPERATIONAL**
**Platform**: https://queoper.netlify.app
**Infrastructure**: Netlify + Neon Database
**Status**: Ready for NYC pilot launch

---

## 📋 **Phase 1: Scale to 50 NYC Hosts (4 weeks)**

### ✅ Week 1: Host Recruitment Strategy
- **Target Categories**: 50 hosts across 5 categories
- **Recruitment Channels**: LinkedIn, Instagram, community partnerships
- **Compensation**: $35-75/hour, 20% platform fee
- **Requirements**: NYC resident 2+ years, expertise verification

### ✅ Week 2-3: Host Onboarding System
- **4-Step Application**: Info → Experience → Availability → Verification
- **Approval Workflow**: Auto-screening → Manual review → Video interview
- **Database Schema**: HostApplications, enhanced Hosts table
- **Training Resources**: Platform tutorial, safety protocols

### ✅ Week 4: Guest Acquisition Campaign
- **Target Audience**: 25-45 NYC professionals, $75K+ income
- **Marketing Channels**: Instagram, TikTok, LinkedIn, Google Ads
- **Campaign**: "Discover Authentic NYC" - Connection, not dating
- **Goal**: 500 registered users, 100 bookings first month

---

## 🎯 **Phase 2: Complete User Experience (6 weeks)**

### ✅ Week 1: User Authentication System
- **Frontend**: Registration/login forms, email verification
- **Backend**: Enhanced user model, password reset, profile management
- **Security**: JWT tokens, bcrypt hashing, email verification
- **API**: 8 authentication endpoints

### ✅ Week 2-3: Host Search & Booking System
- **Search Features**: Text search, category filters, price/location/rating
- **Host Profiles**: Photo gallery, bio, services, availability calendar
- **Booking Flow**: Experience selection → Date/time → Payment → Confirmation
- **Database**: Experiences, enhanced Bookings, HostAvailability tables

### ✅ Week 4: Live Stripe Payment Processing
- **Stripe Connect**: Multi-party payments, host onboarding
- **Payment Flow**: Authorization → Completion → Transfer (80% to host)
- **Security**: PCI compliance, 3D Secure, fraud detection
- **Features**: Refunds, dispute handling, earnings dashboard

### ✅ Week 5-6: Review & Rating System
- **Rating Categories**: Overall, Knowledge, Communication, Value, Authenticity
- **Review Process**: Post-experience prompts, host responses, public display
- **Quality Controls**: Verified bookings only, moderation queue, spam detection
- **Database**: Reviews table with aggregate host ratings

---

## 📱 **Phase 3: Mobile Apps & Advanced Features (10 weeks)**

### ✅ Week 1-8: React Native Mobile Apps
- **Architecture**: Cross-platform, Expo managed workflow
- **Features**: Authentication, discovery, booking, payments, messaging
- **Deployment**: iOS App Store, Google Play Store
- **Timeline**: 8 weeks development + testing

### ✅ Week 9-10: Push Notifications & Location Services
- **Notifications**: Firebase FCM, booking alerts, reminders, messages
- **Location**: Maps integration, nearby hosts, distance calculation
- **Privacy**: Opt-in location, granular permissions, data retention limits
- **Database**: DeviceTokens, Notifications tables

---

## ⚖️ **Phase 4: Business Operations (3 weeks)**

### ✅ Week 1-2: Legal Documentation & Compliance
- **Legal Framework**: Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Host/Guest Agreements
- **Safety Protocols**: Background checks, insurance requirements, emergency procedures
- **Compliance**: Business registration, tax setup, ADA compliance
- **Documents**: 10 legal documents required

### ✅ Week 3: Customer Support & Dispute Resolution
- **Support Channels**: In-app messaging, email, phone, live chat, help center
- **Dispute Process**: Self-resolution → Support mediation → Formal arbitration
- **Performance**: <2hr response, 80% resolved in 24hrs
- **Database**: SupportTickets, SupportMessages, Disputes tables
