# 🎯 Phase 1: NYC Host Recruitment Strategy

## 📋 **Step 1: Host Recruitment Strategy**

### Target Categories (50 Total Hosts)
- **Local Experts** (15 hosts): Food tours, neighborhood guides, hidden gems
- **Cultural Guides** (10 hosts): History, art, music, immigration stories
- **Wellness Coaches** (10 hosts): Meditation, fitness, mindfulness, yoga
- **Creative Mentors** (10 hosts): Art, photography, writing, design
- **Professional Networkers** (5 hosts): Industry connections, career advice

### Recruitment Channels
**Digital Outreach**:
- LinkedIn targeting NYC professionals
- Instagram influencers in each category
- Facebook groups for NYC locals
- Craigslist gigs section
- Nextdoor neighborhood networks

**Community Partnerships**:
- NYC tourism boards
- Local business associations
- Cultural institutions (museums, galleries)
- Fitness studios and wellness centers
- Co-working spaces

### Host Requirements
- **NYC Resident**: Minimum 2 years
- **Expertise**: Demonstrable knowledge/skills in category
- **Availability**: Minimum 10 hours/week
- **Background Check**: Clean record required
- **Insurance**: Professional liability coverage

### Compensation Structure
- **Base Rate**: $35-75/hour depending on category
- **Platform Fee**: 20% to OnPurpose
- **Bonuses**: Performance incentives for high ratings
- **Benefits**: Marketing support, training resources

## 🔧 **Implementation Steps**

### Week 1: Recruitment Launch
1. **Create host application form** with screening questions
2. **Design marketing materials** for each category
3. **Launch social media campaigns** targeting NYC
4. **Partner outreach** to local organizations

### Week 2-3: Application Processing
1. **Review applications** and conduct video interviews
2. **Background checks** and reference verification
3. **Skills assessment** for each category
4. **Onboarding training** for approved hosts

### Week 4: Host Activation
1. **Profile creation** with photos and descriptions
2. **Calendar setup** and availability scheduling
3. **Pricing optimization** based on market research
4. **Launch host profiles** on platform

**Target: 50 verified hosts within 30 days! 🚀**
